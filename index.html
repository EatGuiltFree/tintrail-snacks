Our story

Roasted with care.
Packed with purpose.

TinTrail was born from a simple frustration — great snacks came in terrible packaging. Stale pouches that spill, airport kiosks charging ₹450 for ordinary nuts.

We source the finest nuts, seeds and grains directly, roast each batch in small quantities, and pack them into beautiful food-grade tins that respect both product and planet.
